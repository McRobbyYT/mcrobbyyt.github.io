Why there's strict verification

Because AI bots are everywhere now, running a website is way harder than it used to be.
What used to be a place to share stuff has turned into a fight with bots that keep stealing and reusing my work without asking.

Since these bots won’t quit, I added strict verification so they can’t get to other pages.
I don’t love doing this, but after seeing my work pop up in random places without credit, I felt I had to.

Sorry if this is annoying.
